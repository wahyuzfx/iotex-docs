# Transfer Token On Erc20 Contract

Usage: ioctl xrc20 transfer ALIAS|TARGET_ADDRESS AMOUNT -c ALIAS|CONTRACT_ADDRESS [-l GAS_LIMIT] -s SIGNER [-p GAS_PRICE]
